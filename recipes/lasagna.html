Lasagna


Description

A classic Italian baked pasta dish featuring layers of tender lasagna sheets, savory meat sauce (bolognese), rich and creamy cheese mixture, and melted mozzarella.


Prep Time: 20 minutes | Cook Time: 45 minutes | Servings: 6 to 8


Ingredients

  1. The Layers
    • Pasta Sheets : 12
    • Cheese Topping : 2 cups shredded mozzarella + ½ cup grated Parmesan
  2. The Meat Sauce
    • Ground Beef : 450g
    • Small Onion : 1, diced
    • Garlic : 2 cloves, minced
    • Tomato Sauce : 1 can (680g)
    • Italian Seasoning : 1 tsp
  3. The Cheese Sauce
    • Ricotta cheese : 425g
    • Egg : 1, beaten
    • Parmesan : 1/4 cup, grated
    • A handful of Parsley, chopped

Instructions

  1. Prep the elements
    • The Sauce : Brown the ground meat with onions and garlic in a skillet. Drain excess fat, stir in the marinara sauce and Italian seasoning, and let it simmer for 5 minutes.
    • The Cheese : In a bowl, mix the ricotta, egg, Parmesan, and parsley together until smooth.
  2. Assemble the lasagna
    • Sauce : Spread a thin layer on the bottom.
    • Pasta Sheets : Lay 3 to 4 sheets side-by-side.
    • Ricotta : Spread a portion of the ricotta mixture over the noodles.
    • Mozzarella : Sprinkle a handful of mozzarella.
    • Repeat these steps 3 more times, ending with a layer of noodles topped with the remaining meat sauce, mozzarella, and the extra Parmesan.
  3. Bake to perfection
    • Preheat your oven to 190°C.
    • Cover the dish with foil (tent it slightly so it doesn't stick to the cheese) and bake for 25 minutes.
    • Remove the foil and bake uncovered for another 15 minutes until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
