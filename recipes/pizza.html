Pepperoni Pizza


Description

A crisp, golden homemade crust topped with a zesty tomato sauce, gooey melted mozzarella cheese, and spicy, savory pepperoni slices.


Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 12 minutes | Servings: 3 to 4


Ingredients

  1. The Crust
    • Pizza Dough : 1 pre-made ball (or store-bought)
    • Cornmeal : 1 tbsp (for the baking sheet)
  2. The Toppings
    • Pizza Sauce : 1/2 cup
    • Shredded Mozzarella : 1.5 cups
    • Pepperoni Slices : 15 to 20 slices
    • Dried Oregano : 1/2 tsp

Instructions

  1. Prep the Oven and Dough
    • Preheat your oven to 230°C (450°F). If using a pizza stone, place it inside while heating.
    • Roll or stretch the pizza dough out on a lightly floured surface into a 12-inch circle.
  2. Assemble the Pizza
    • Base : Dust a baking sheet with cornmeal and transfer the stretched dough onto it.
    • Sauce : Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust.
    • Cheese & Toppings : Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce, layer the pepperoni slices on top, and dust with dried oregano.
  3. Bake to Perfection
    • Bake at 230°C for 10 to 12 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbling.
    • Let it cool for 2 minutes before slicing.
