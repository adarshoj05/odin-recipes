Spaghetti Bolognese


Description

A quick and comforting weekday staple featuring perfectly al dente spaghetti tossed in a rich, deeply savory seasoned meat sauce.


Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 20 minutes | Servings: 4


Ingredients

  1. The Pasta
    • Spaghetti Noodles : 400g
    • Salt : 1 tbsp (for boiling water)
  2. The Meat Sauce
    • Ground Beef : 400g
    • Garlic : 2 cloves, minced
    • Marinara/Tomato Sauce : 1 jar (680g)
    • Italian Seasoning : 1 tsp
    • Grated Parmesan : 1/4 cup (for serving)

Instructions

  1. Cook the Pasta
    • Bring a large pot of salted water to a rolling boil.
    • Add the spaghetti and cook for 8 to 10 minutes (or according to package instructions) until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Simmer the Sauce
    • While the pasta cooks, brown the ground beef and minced garlic in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Drain excess grease.
    • Pour in the tomato sauce and Italian seasoning. Stir well and let it simmer over low heat for 10 minutes.
  3. Combine and Serve
    • Add the drained spaghetti directly into the skillet with the sauce and toss gently to coat the noodles evenly.
    • Serve hot with a generous sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese on top.
